Who We Are

ReCent DST Limited (Research Centre for Development in Science and Technology SL Ltd) is a pioneering multidisciplinary research, development, and technology company founded in 2017.

Born from the critical need for research-driven innovation in developing countries, we bridge the gap between scientific knowledge and practical solutions that improve lives and communities.

Our company brings together science and engineering experts from Sierra Leone, the broader West African region, and the global diaspora. This unique combination of local insight and international expertise enables us to deliver world-class research and technology solutions that are culturally relevant and globally competitive.

Our Story: From Vision to Impact

The journey of innovation and growth that defines ReCent DST Limited

Founding Vision

Established in 2017, ReCent DST emerged from a clear recognition: developing countries need robust research and development infrastructure to drive sustainable growth and innovation. Our founders, a collective of multidisciplinary experts, saw an opportunity to create a company that would not only conduct rigorous research but also translate findings into actionable solutions for governments, businesses, and civil society organizations.

Growth & Evolution

From our early days focusing on market research and basic web development, we have evolved into a comprehensive research and technology hub. Today, we manage over 30 ongoing digital projects, have built more than 50 websites, and continue to expand our research capabilities across multiple disciplines. Our growth reflects our commitment to excellence and our clients' trust in our capabilities.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable source of results-based data and information to steer national development across Africa, while serving as a bridge between local innovation and global technological advancement.

Leading Africa's Research Revolution

Our Mission

To innovate and build reliable data resources for national and international development through:

  • Conducting rigorous qualitative and quantitative research
  • Developing cutting-edge digital solutions
  • Providing strategic consulting based on evidence
  • Building local capacity with international standards
  • Fostering collaboration between research and practice
